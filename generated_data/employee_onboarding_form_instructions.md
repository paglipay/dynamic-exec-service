# Employee Onboarding Form Instructions

Welcome to the employee onboarding form. Please follow the instructions below to complete the form accurately.

## How to Fill Out the Form

1. **Employee Name**: Enter the full legal name of the new employee.
2. **Start Date**: Provide the employee's official start date in YYYY-MM-DD format.
3. **Department**: Select the department the employee will be joining from the dropdown menu.
4. **Email Address**: Enter the employee’s company email address.
5. **Job Title**: Specify the official job title.
6. **Manager Name**: Enter the name of the employee’s direct manager.
7. **Work Location**: Provide the office location or remote status.
8. **Phone Number**: Enter a contact phone number.
9. **Emergency Contact**: Provide the name and phone number of an emergency contact.
10. **Skills or Experience**: List relevant skills or prior work experience.
11. **Equipment Needs**: Specify any equipment or tools the employee will need.

Please review your input carefully before submitting the form.

Thank you!
